How PactPool Works

1

Lock ETH with a Goal

Choose a commitment tier, category, write your goal publicly, and set a lock period (1–365 days). Your ETH goes into the smart contract.

2

Your Goal Goes Public

Your goal appears on the Goal Wall, visible to everyone. Accountability is built into the protocol — not just peer pressure.

3

Complete on Time → Earn Bonus

Reach your unlock date? Withdraw your full ETH + a proportional share of the penalty pool. Others' failures fund your success.

4

Exit Early → Pay the Pool

Withdraw before your unlock date and pay a 10–30% dynamic penalty. The further from unlock, the higher the penalty. Automatic, immutable.
